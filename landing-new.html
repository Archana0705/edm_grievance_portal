About the EDM Grievance Portal

The EDM Grievance Redressal Portal is a centralized platform designed to empower citizens, streamline operations, and enhance accountability across all levels of governance. Built on transparency, efficiency, and inclusivity, it ensures timely redressal of grievances and smooth coordination among stakeholders.

  • Citizen-Centric: Easy grievance registration and real-time tracking.
  • Efficient Operators: Seamless support through eSevai operators and helplines.
  • Department Accountability: Clear workflows and defined responsibility channels.
  • Data-Driven Monitoring: Insights and analytics for eDMs & TNeGA officials.
